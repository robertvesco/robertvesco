The Dark Visions of Techno Optimists

2025-03-20
#  # 

Techno-optimists tell us we can’t stop progress and that we needn’t worry because technology creates problems, but it can solve them too. Trust. Stop resisting. It’s like we are suffering from a collective Stockholm Syndrome. But the problem is most people haven’t really heard what they are saying. And among those who have, instead of coming up with alternatives they are finding solace in Gods and ancient philosophies. We can do better. I’ll touch on that in future articles, but for now, check out this quotes.

Abundance, prosperity, freedom is the world we are being sold. These are the be all and end all. Constraints are bad.
